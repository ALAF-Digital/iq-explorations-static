Years of experience

They say I was born to travel, I had my first steps as a child when I was 9 months old on an airplane from Amman to Kuwait! That could explain my passion for travel and exploring new places and guiding others to the same steps in discovering this world!

I visited more than 130 cities and more than 34 countries and still counting. There is so much to see an discover. The more you travel, the more humble you become as you get to see how small you are in front of this massive universe!

I am a Jordanian Palestinian, who lived and worked in UAE for most of my life. Since I was born, traveling was a constant part of my life, which I started to admire more over time. It taught me a lot, as what you see in books is not close to what you observe in real life!

I, also, take joy in photography and videography. To wait for the right moment in order to capture the perfect photograph..

Come and take a journey with me!
