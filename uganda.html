5 nights trip to Uganda

Embark on once in a lifetime unique experience in the heart of Africa with Gorilla Tracking in Bwindi National Park. During your visit, will be able to enjoy Batwa Cultural Experience, the famous trip that is known to be the forest keeper to learn about and interact with the local Batwa people.

The trip will be taken off by air from Entebbe on the next morning of the group arrival.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Entebbe International Airport and transfer to the hotel where you will spend the night.
Day 2: An early morning flight from Entebbe to Kihihi and transfer to the Camp.
Later on, a visit to Batwa Tribe.
Day 3: Gorilla Tracking experience with expert guide.
Day 4: Catch the flight back to Entebbe.

  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner meals during the stay in the Camp.
  • Breakfast meal during the stay in Entebbe.
  • Domestic flight between Entebbe and Kihihi
  • Airport transfer on arrival and departure
